## ğŸ“ˆ Analysis Components

### 1. Liquidity Ratios
- **Working Capital**: Net current assets available
- **Current Ratio**: Ability to pay short-term obligations
- **Quick Ratio**: Liquidity excluding inventory
- **Cash Ratio**: Most conservative liquidity measure

### 2. Turnover Ratios
- **Total Asset Turnover**: Efficiency in using assets to generate revenue
- **Fixed Asset Turnover**: Productivity of long-term assets
- **Accounts Receivable Turnover**: Collection efficiency
- **Inventory Turnover**: Inventory management effectiveness
- **Accounts Payable Turnover**: Payment practices

### 3. Solvency Ratios
- **Times Interest Earned**: Ability to meet interest obligations
- **Debt Ratio**: Proportion of assets financed by debt
- **Debt-to-Equity Ratio**: Financial leverage
- **Debt-to-Tangible Net Worth**: Debt relative to tangible equity
